## 2. Rotation Matrix by Platform

### Instagram Guidelines
- **Quantity:** 5 to 10 hashtags.
- **Mix:** 1 Brand + 3 High-Volume + 3 Mid-Volume + 2 Niche.
- **Placement:** Place at the very end of the caption separated by a line break, or in the first comment.

### LinkedIn Guidelines
- **Quantity:** 3 to 5 hashtags.
- **Mix:** 1 Brand + 2 Category + 1 Topic-Specific.
- **Placement:** Integrates cleanly at the end of the post.

### Facebook Guidelines
- **Quantity:** 2 to 4 hashtags.
- **Mix:** Focus on family/local category hashtags (e.g. `#MomsOfIndia`, `#HealthyBreakfast`).
