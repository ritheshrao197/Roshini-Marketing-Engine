**Recipe: Sprouted Millet & Date Energy Bites**

These no-bake energy bites are quick, nutritious, and perfect for school lunchboxes, after-school snacks, or a quick office desk bite. They utilize the wholesome power of Roshini's Nutrimix and natural sweetness from dates.

**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Chill Time:** 30 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Yields:** Approx. 12-15 bites

**Ingredients:**
*   1 cup Roshini's Nutrimix (contains sprouted millets, nuts & dry fruits)
*   1 cup Medjool dates, pitted (approx. 15-20 dates)
*   2 tbsp unsweetened almond butter (or any nut butter of choice)
*   1 tbsp coconut oil (melted, optional, for extra moisture)
*   1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
*   Pinch of sea salt
*   2 tbsp finely chopped almonds or cashews (for texture and rolling, optional)
*   2 tbsp desiccated coconut (for rolling, optional)

**Instructions:**

1.  **Prepare Dates:** If your dates are very dry, soak them in warm water for 5-10 minutes, then drain thoroughly before pitting. This makes them easier to blend.
2.  **Blend Wet Ingredients:** In a food processor, combine the pitted dates, almond butter, melted coconut oil (if using), vanilla extract, and sea salt. Process until a thick, sticky paste forms. You might need to scrape down the sides a few times.
3.  **Add Nutrimix:** Add the Roshini's Nutrimix to the food processor. Process again until the Nutrimix is fully incorporated and the mixture comes together to form a cohesive, slightly crumbly dough. If it's too dry, add a tiny bit more coconut oil or a teaspoon of water; if it's too sticky, add a little more Nutrimix or oats.
4.  **Form the Bites:** Transfer the dough to a bowl. Take small portions (about 1 tablespoon each) and roll them between your palms to form uniform balls.
5.  **Coat (Optional):** If desired, roll the energy bites in finely chopped nuts or desiccated coconut for an extra layer of flavor and texture.
6.  **Chill:** Place the finished energy bites on a plate lined with parchment paper. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to help them firm up and hold their shape.
7.  **Serve & Store:** Enjoy these nutritious bites cold! Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week, or freeze for longer storage.

**Pro Tip:** For extra protein, you can add 1 tablespoon of plant-based protein powder (unflavored) along with the Nutrimix in step 3. Remember, Roshini's Nutrimix contains nuts.
