Children’s brains are incredible engines of growth and development. Every lesson learned, every game played, every new skill acquired depends on a steady supply of essential nutrients. A well-nourished brain can lead to improved concentration, better memory, enhanced problem-solving skills, and sustained energy throughout the school day. *   **Almonds:** These versatile nuts are a natural source of Vitamin E, which is known for its antioxidant properties. They also contain healthy fats and protein, contributing to a feeling of fullness and steady energy.

*   **Cardamom:** Beyond its delightful aroma, cardamom aids digestion and can make meals more palatable, which is especially helpful for picky eaters.

**Recipe: Nutrimix Power Bites (No-Bake)**

These quick, no-cook power bites are perfect for lunchboxes, after-school snacks, or even a quick breakfast on the go. Packed with natural energy and brain-boosting goodness!

**Yields:** 12-15 bites
**Prep time:** 10 minutes
**Cook time:** 0 minutes (plus 30 mins chill time)

**Ingredients:**
*   ½ cup Roshini's Nutrimix (plain, unsweetened)
*   ½ cup pitted dates, firmly packed
*   ¼ cup mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews), finely chopped or pulsed
*   1 tbsp ghee (clarified butter), melted
*   ½ tsp cardamom powder (optional, for extra flavor)
*   2-3 tbsp warm water (if needed, to bind)
*   2 tbsp desiccated coconut or powdered dry fruits for rolling (optional)

**Instructions:**

1.  **Prepare Dates:** If your dates are hard, soak them in warm water for 5-10 minutes, then drain thoroughly. In a food processor, combine the pitted dates and pulse until they form a sticky paste.
2.  **Combine Ingredients:** In a mixing bowl, add the date paste, Nutrimix, finely chopped nuts, melted ghee, and cardamom powder (if using).
3.  **Mix Thoroughly:** Use your hands to mix everything together until a cohesive dough forms. It should be sticky but pliable. If the mixture feels too dry, add warm water one tablespoon at a time until it comes together. If it's too sticky, add a little more Nutrimix.
4.  **Roll into Bites:** Take small portions of the mixture (about 1 tablespoon each) and roll them between your palms to form round, compact bites.
5.  **Coat (Optional):** If desired, roll the formed bites in desiccated coconut or powdered dry fruits for an extra layer of flavor and to prevent sticking.
6.  **Chill:** Place the Nutrimix Power Bites on a plate or in an airtight container and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. This helps them firm up and hold their shape.
7.  **Serve & Store:** Enjoy immediately or pack them in lunchboxes. Store any leftover bites in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5-7 days.

**Tip:** For extra crunch, lightly toast the nuts before chopping!
