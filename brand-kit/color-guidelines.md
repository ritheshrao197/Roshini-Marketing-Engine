# Roshini's Home Products — Brand Kit

## Identity
- **Brand:** Roshini's Home Products
- **Tagline:** Homemade Goodness for a Healthier Family
- **Category:** Homemade Millet & Dry Fruit Nutrition Products
- **Brand Mission:** Help every family adopt healthier eating habits using traditional ingredients prepared with modern hygiene standards.

## Voice & Tone
- Warm and family friendly
- Trustworthy
- Educational
- Honest
- Science-backed but easy to understand
- Never overpromise health benefits
- Encourage healthy lifestyles rather than miracle claims

## Target Audience
- Parents
- Working Professionals
- Fitness Enthusiasts
- Pregnant Women
- Senior Citizens
- Kids
- People looking for healthy breakfast options

## Content Pillars
1. **Nutrition Education:** Benefits of Millets, Dry Fruits, Seeds, Vitamins, Protein, Fiber
2. **Product Awareness:** Nutrimix, Homemade Process, Premium Ingredients, No Preservatives, No Added Sugar
3. **Healthy Recipes:** Nutrimix Malt, Smoothies, Protein Bowls, Healthy Desserts, Millet Recipes
4. **Lifestyle:** Healthy Morning Routine, Meal Planning, Family Nutrition, Fitness, Kids Health
5. **Customer Stories:** Reviews, Testimonials, User Recipes, Transformation Stories

## Formatting Rules by Platform

### LinkedIn
- Educational and story-driven
- 150–250 words
- One nutrition insight
- One actionable takeaway
- End with a question
- 3–5 hashtags

### Instagram
- Caption 80–150 words
- Hook in first sentence
- CTA (Call to Action)
- 5–10 hashtags

### Facebook
- Family focused
- Recipe or health tip
- Encourage comments

### WhatsApp Status
- Under 40 words
- Simple and emotional
- Accompanied by a product image / reference

### Email Newsletter
- Include:
  - Weekly nutrition tip
  - Healthy recipe
  - Product spotlight
  - Customer testimonial
  - Offer (optional)
