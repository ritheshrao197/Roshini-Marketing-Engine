# Roshini's Home Products — Visual Style Guide

This style guide outlines the visual principles, design standards, and layout guidelines for Roshini's Home Products. It ensures consistent branding across Canva graphics, packaging mockups, website designs, and social media media.

---

## 1. Visual Brand Aesthetic
- **Mood:** Warm, rustic, organic, wholesome, family-friendly, and pure.
- **Imagery Style:** 
  - *Lighting:* Soft, natural daylight. Avoid harsh flash or overly processed artificial studio lighting.
  - *Backdrops:* Textured light-colored woods, linen fabrics, slate, stone, or clay tiles.
  - *Garnishes & Props:* Scattered raw millet grains, almonds, cardamom pods, walnuts, fresh mint leaves, or clay bowls/terracotta cups.
  - *Color Overtones:* Gentle earthen warm tones blending with natural greens and golden millet accents.

---

## 2. Typography & Hierarchy
Ensure these fonts are configured in Canva templates and web landing pages:

- **Display & Headings:** **Poppins Bold**
  - *Usage:* Carousel headers, cover graphics, main website sections, blog titles.
  - *Feel:* Clean, friendly, geometric, readable at small sizes.
- **Body Text:** **Poppins Regular / Medium**
  - *Usage:* Subtitles, checklist points, slide descriptions, ingredient lists.
  - *Feel:* Legible, clean, modern.
- **Accent & Pull-Quotes:** **Playfair Display (Italic)**
  - *Usage:* Testimonials, motherly quotes, traditional wisdom callouts, seasonal taglines.
  - *Feel:* Classic, elegant, heritage-inspired.

---

## 3. Brand Color Palette (Hex Codes)
Always use these exact brand colors to maintain visual consistency:

| Color Role | Hex Code | Visual Style |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary Green** | `#4E7A2E` | Natural leaf green, represents purity and organic growth. |
| **Accent Gold** | `#D98C2B` | Millet gold, represents wholesome grains, harvest, and warmth. |
| **Warm Sand** | `#FFF8EE` | Warm ivory/sand background. Avoid pure stark white backgrounds. |
| **Dark Charcoal** | `#4A4A4A` | Soft, readable charcoal for all primary text (replaces pure black). |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Used for cards, surface shapes, and text on dark backgrounds. |
| **Leaf Success** | `#5FA146` | Highlights, benefit checkmarks, and positive indicators. |

---

## 4. Canva Graphic Layout Frameworks

### Dimensions & Ratios
- **Instagram / Facebook Feeds:** 1:1 Square (1080 x 1080 px)
- **Instagram Carousels:** 4:5 Portrait (1080 x 1350 px) — *Highly recommended for readability.*
- **Reel Covers & Stories:** 9:16 Vertical (1080 x 1920 px)

### Layout Principles
1. **The 30% Negative Space Rule:** Keep at least 30% of the graphic clean and free of text. Use a solid Warm Sand `#FFF8EE` background or clean wooden textures.
2. **Minimalist Borders:** Use thin, natural green `#4E7A2E` borders or organic leaf outlines. Avoid thick, blocky frames.
3. **Contrast:** Dark Charcoal `#4A4A4A` or Primary Green `#4E7A2E` text must always sit on a Warm Sand `#FFF8EE` surface to ensure readability.
4. **Icons & Checkmarks:** Use simple hand-drawn or minimal outline icons. Use success checkmarks (`#5FA146`) for list infographics.
