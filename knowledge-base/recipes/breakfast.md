# Recipe Directory: Healthy Breakfast Options

A nutritious breakfast provides the foundation for energy, focus, and digestion throughout the day. Here are easy, wholesome breakfast recipes using Roshini's products.

---

## 1. The Classic Nutrimix Porridge (Malt)
*The traditional daily breakfast bowl. Creamy, filling, and comforting.*

- **Prep Time:** 2 minutes | **Cook Time:** 5–7 minutes | **Servings:** 1
- **Ideal For:** Kids, Seniors, Pregnant Women, Busy Adults.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 1 cup (250ml) Milk or Water (or a 50/50 mix)
  - 1 teaspoon organic jaggery powder or honey (optional sweetener)
  - A small pinch of cardamom powder (optional, for extra aroma)
  - 1–2 chopped almonds or pistachios for garnish
- **Method:**
  1. **Dissolve:** Add the Nutrimix powder to a cold saucepan. Pour in the milk/water and stir/whisk vigorously to dissolve all powder and eliminate lumps *before* placing on heat.
  2. **Cook:** Place the pan on a low-medium flame. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes, stirring continuously to prevent the porridge from sticking or forming lumps as it thickens.
  3. **Finish:** Once it thickens into a rich, custard-like porridge, turn off the flame.
  4. **Sweeten & Serve:** Stir in jaggery or honey. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve warm.

## 2. Dry Fruit & Sprouted Millet Porridge
*A denser, richer breakfast bowl packed with healthy fats.*

- **Prep Time:** 2 minutes | **Cook Time:** 5 minutes | **Servings:** 1
- **Ideal For:** Growing children, pregnant women, and underweight individuals.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 250ml full-cream milk
  - 1 teaspoon pure cow's ghee (clarified butter)
  - 1 tablespoon date paste or chopped soft dates
  - 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts and almonds
- **Method:**
  1. Whisk the Nutrimix powder into the cold milk until completely smooth.
  2. Cook on low flame for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Stir in date paste and ghee just before turning off the heat.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve warm.

## 3. Nutrimix Protein Oats
*A modern twist merging traditional millets with rolled oats.*

- **Prep Time:** 2 minutes | **Cook Time:** 6 minutes | **Servings:** 1
- **Ideal For:** Gym goers and fitness enthusiasts.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 3 tablespoons rolled oats
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix** (dry-roasted for 2 minutes and cooled)
  - 300ml almond milk or cow's milk
  - 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  - 1/2 sliced banana or fresh berries
- **Method:**
  1. Add rolled oats and milk to a saucepan. Cook on medium heat for 3 minutes until oats are soft.
  2. Whisk the roasted Nutrimix powder with a splash of water, then stir it into the cooking oats.
  3. Simmer together for another 3 minutes on low flame, stirring to prevent sticking.
  4. Pour into a bowl, stir in chia seeds, and top with banana slices or fresh berries.
