# Recipe Directory: Savory & Travel Snacks

For mid-day hunger, evening tea accompaniments, or summer cooling, healthy snacks are essential. Here are savory and quick snack options.

---

## 1. Savory Buttermilk Nutrimix Porridge
*A cooling, digestive drink perfect for hot summers and weight management.*

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes | **Cook Time:** 3 minutes | **Servings:** 1
- **Ideal For:** Diabetics, Weight Managers, Working Professionals.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 1 cup (250ml) Water
  - 1/2 cup sour curd / buttermilk
  - A pinch of roasted cumin (jeera) powder
  - A pinch of pink salt or rock salt
  - 1 teaspoon finely chopped coriander and curry leaves for garnish
- **Method:**
  1. **Cook:** Whisk Nutrimix with water in a saucepan until lump-free. Cook on medium-low flame for 3 minutes until thick. Remove from flame and let it cool completely to room temperature.
  2. **Mix curd:** Whisk buttermilk/curd with salt and cumin powder until smooth.
  3. **Combine:** Stir the cooled millet porridge into the seasoned buttermilk. Mix thoroughly.
  4. **Garnish:** Garnish with chopped coriander and curry leaves. Serve cold for a refreshing, low-GI beverage or light lunch.

## 2. Savory Nutrimix Crackers
*Crispy baked crackers perfect with tea or a healthy dip.*

- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes | **Bake Time:** 15 minutes | **Yield:** 15–20 crackers
- **Ideal For:** Travel snacks, school lunchboxes.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1 cup **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 1/4 cup whole wheat flour (or gluten-free oats flour)
  - 2 tablespoons oil or butter
  - 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  - 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes or pepper powder
  - Salt to taste
  - Warm water to knead
- **Method:**
  1. **Mix dry ingredients:** In a bowl, combine Nutrimix, wheat flour, ajwain, salt, and spices.
  2. **Add fat:** Add oil/butter and rub it into the flour with fingertips until it resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. **Knead:** Add warm water slowly and knead into a tight, firm dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. **Roll & Cut:** Roll out the dough thinly on a baking sheet. Cut into small squares or circles using a cookie cutter or knife. Prick each cracker with a fork.
  5. **Bake:** Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 12–15 minutes until golden and crisp. Let them cool completely before storing in an airtight jar.
