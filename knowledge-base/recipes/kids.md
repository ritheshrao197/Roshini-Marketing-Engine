# Recipe Directory: Kids' Special Nutrition

Growing children need nutrient-dense meals that are easy to digest, taste delicious, and support bone and brain development. These curated recipes are child-tested and approved.

---

## 1. Sweet Millet Malt (Toddler's Daily Porridge)
*A sweet, nutrient-rich daily porridge that supports growth and strong bones.*

- **Prep Time:** 2 minutes | **Cook Time:** 5–7 minutes | **Age Group:** 8+ months
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 1 cup (250ml) whole milk or water
  - 1 teaspoon pure cow's ghee (clarified butter)
  - 1 teaspoon organic jaggery powder or 1 tablespoon date paste
- **Method:**
  1. Whisk the Nutrimix powder into the cold milk/water until completely smooth.
  2. Cook on low flame for 5–7 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent lumps.
  3. Turn off the flame, then stir in the ghee and jaggery powder or date paste.
  4. Serve warm. *Tip: The addition of ghee improves the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (like Vitamin E) from nuts.*

## 2. Healthy Dry Fruit Laddus (Energy Balls)
*An excellent sugar-free snack box alternative to commercial chocolates.*

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes | **Cook Time:** 5 minutes | **Age Group:** 1+ years
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1 cup **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 1/2 cup soft seedless dates (mashed or ground)
  - 2 tablespoons pure cow's ghee
  - 1 tablespoon chopped nuts (almonds, cashews)
- **Method:**
  1. Dry roast the Nutrimix on a low flame for 4 minutes until warm and fragrant. Let it cool.
  2. In a small pan, warm the ghee and sauté the mashed dates on low heat for 1–2 minutes until soft.
  3. Combine the roasted Nutrimix, warm date paste, and chopped nuts in a bowl.
  4. Roll into small, kid-friendly balls. Store in a zip-lock pouch or jar.

## 3. Double Chocolate Millet Pudding
*A creamy, chocolate-flavored pudding that makes millets fun for kids.*

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes | **Cook Time:** 8 minutes | **Age Group:** 1.5+ years
- **Ingredients:**
  - 3 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix**
  - 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  - 300ml milk
  - 2 tablespoons jaggery or palm sugar
  - A tiny pinch of cinnamon
- **Method:**
  1. Whisk the Nutrimix, cocoa powder, and milk together in a cold pot.
  2. Cook on low flame for 8 minutes, stirring constantly as it thickens.
  3. Turn off the heat, stir in jaggery and cinnamon.
  4. Pour into small cups and chill before serving.
