# Recipe Directory: Smoothies & Shakes

For hot mornings, quick post-workout recovery, or busy office prep, raw blending is the easiest way to consume sprouted nutrition. Here are quick, no-cook smoothie recipes using Roshini's products.

---

## 1. Classic Nutrimix Banana & Date Smoothie
*A fast, cold, nutrient-dense shake requiring no cooking.*

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes | **Servings:** 1
- **Ideal For:** Working Professionals, Gym Goers, Busy Corporate Workers.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix** (dry-roasted in a pan for 2 minutes and cooled)
  - 1 ripe Banana (preferably frozen for a creamy texture)
  - 3–4 soft, pitted dates (soaked in warm water for 5 minutes to soften)
  - 1 glass (250ml) chilled milk (cow's milk, soy milk, or almond milk)
  - A tiny pinch of cinnamon powder
  - 1 teaspoon pumpkin seeds for topping
- **Method:**
  1. **Prepare:** Soak dates in warm water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  2. **Blend:** Add the banana slices, softened dates, chilled milk, roasted Nutrimix, and cinnamon powder to a blender.
  3. **Liquefy:** Blend on high speed for 1 minute until completely smooth, frothy, and uniform.
  4. **Serve:** Pour into a tall glass, sprinkle pumpkin seeds on top, and serve cold.

## 2. Tropical Mango & Nutrimix Shake
*A seasonal, creamy breakfast shake.*

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes | **Servings:** 1
- **Ideal For:** Children, office snacks, and summer breakfasts.
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 tablespoons **Roshini's Nutrimix** (roasted and cooled)
  - 1 cup fresh ripe mango pulp (Alphonso or Kesar)
  - 250ml cold coconut milk or dairy milk
  - 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  - A pinch of cardamom powder
- **Method:**
  1. Blend the fresh mango pulp, milk, roasted Nutrimix, honey, and cardamom powder together in a blender.
  2. Process on high speed until thick and smooth.
  3. Serve chilled in a glass garnished with chopped mango chunks.
