# Quality Standards, Hygiene & FSSAI Guidelines

This document details the food safety protocols, sourcing standards, and regulatory compliance followed by Roshini's Home Products.

---

## 1. Sourcing Quality Standards

We source all raw materials from certified local farms and trusted vendors:
- **Organic Millets:** 100% organic, pesticide-free grains sourced from dryland farmers in Karnataka and Tamil Nadu (Ragi, Bajra, Jowar, Foxtail, Little millets).
- **Premium Dry Fruits:** Grade-A premium almonds (Mamra/Gurbandi), high-oil content walnuts, and unsalted pistachios. No broken pieces or processing leftovers are used.
- **Natural Spices & Flavorings:** Premium green cardamom pods from Idukki, Kerala, selected for high essential oil content and natural aroma.

---

## 2. Food Safety & Hygiene Protocol

All products are manufactured under clean-room conditions at our home-production facility:
- **Sanitization:** All roasting pans, mixing bowls, and stone grinders are thoroughly washed, sanitized, and completely dried before preparing each batch.
- **Protective Gear:** Handlers must wear hairnets, gloves, and clean aprons during the entire drying, roasting, grinding, and packing process.
- **Sprouting Hygiene:** Soaking and sprouting are carried out in food-grade stainless steel containers. Water is filtered (RO) to prevent contamination during the 12-hour germination phase.
- **Packaging:** Moisture-proof, food-grade stand-up pouches with zip locks are heat-sealed immediately after grinding to preserve flavor and lock out humidity.

---

## 3. Regulatory Compliance & Guidelines (FSSAI)

To maintain standards and prepare for food safety inspections:
- **FSSAI Status:** Operating under the FSSAI Registration category for home-based food businesses (micro-retailer/petty food manufacturer).
- **Labeling Requirements:** Every pack must display:
  - Complete list of ingredients.
  - Allergen warnings (e.g., "Contains Tree Nuts" / "Contains Peanuts").
  - Manufacture date, Batch Number, and Expiry Date (6 months from packaging).
  - Clear nutritional facts panel (calcium, iron, protein, carbohydrates, fats, energy).
  - FSSAI Registration number and contact email.
- **Responsible Claims Policy:** Avoid claiming that the product "cures" or "prevents" medical conditions. Frame benefits around nutrition, healthy lifestyle, slow-release energy, and natural development support.
