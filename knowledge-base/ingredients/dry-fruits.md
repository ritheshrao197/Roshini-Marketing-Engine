# Ingredient Directory: Premium Nuts & Dry Fruits

We use only Grade-A premium nuts and dry fruits in our health mixes. They provide natural healthy fats, plant protein, essential vitamins, and a rich, creamy mouthfeel without requiring milk solids or artificial thickeners.

---

## 1. Almond (Badam)
- **Grade/Sourcing:** Mamra and Gurbandi almonds selected for high natural oil and vitamin content.
- **Key Nutrients:** Vitamin E, Monounsaturated Fatty Acids (MUFAs), Magnesium, and Riboflavin.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Cognitive Development:** Promotes brain health and memory retention, making it essential for growing school-age children.
  - **Skin & Hair:** High Vitamin E content provides strong antioxidant support for healthy skin and hair.
  - **Sustained Energy:** Healthy fats and protein keep energy levels elevated without crashes.

## 2. Cashew (Kaju)
- **Sourcing:** Premium whole white cashews. No processing waste or broken pieces.
- **Key Nutrients:** Copper, Zinc, Magnesium, and healthy unsaturated fats.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Immune Support:** Zinc and copper play a critical role in cellular repair and immune response.
  - **Rich Mouthfeel:** Gives cooked porridges and cold smoothies a naturally smooth, creamy texture.

## 3. Pistachio (Pista)
- **Sourcing:** Unsalted, premium green pistachios.
- **Key Nutrients:** Vitamin B6, Potassium, Lutein, and antioxidants.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Vision Health:** Lutein and Zeaxanthin protect eyes from blue light and oxidative damage.
  - **Vascular Support:** Supports clean arteries and optimal blood flow.

## 4. Walnut (Akhrot)
- **Sourcing:** High-oil content premium walnut halves.
- **Key Nutrients:** Omega-3 fatty acids (Alpha-Linolenic Acid / ALA), plant sterols, and antioxidants.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Brain Structure:** ALA Omega-3 is a key building block for brain membranes and cognitive sharpness.
  - **Heart Protection:** Reduces cellular inflammation and supports cholesterol management.

## 5. Dates (Khajur)
- **Sourcing:** Soft, premium seedless dates.
- **Key Nutrients:** Natural fruit sugars (fructose & glucose), iron, potassium, and dietary fiber.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Natural Sweetener:** Used as a sugar alternative to sweeten mixes naturally.
  - **Iron Supply:** Supports red blood cell production, helping pregnant women and kids fight fatigue.
  - **Digestion:** Soluble fiber prevents constipation.

## 6. Peanut (Shengdana)
- **Sourcing:** Clean, slow-roasted red skin peanuts.
- **Key Nutrients:** High plant protein, Resveratrol, Niacin, and healthy fats.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Muscle Recovery:** Excellent affordable source of vegetarian protein for muscle repair and growth.
  - **Metabolic Support:** Niacin helps process nutrients into active bodily energy.
