# Ingredient Directory: Natural Sweeteners & Sweetening Policies

At Roshini's Home Products, our core philosophy is to keep our products as close to nature as possible. For this reason, we adhere to strict sweetening policies.

---

## 1. Our Sugar Policy

- **0% Added Refined Sugar:** We never add white sugar, brown sugar, high-fructose corn syrup, or artificial chemical sweeteners (like aspartame or sucralose) to our standard mixes.
- **Zero Fillers:** We never use maltodextrin, starch, or dextrose to add bulk or fake sweetness.
- **Natural Baseline:** The sweet undertones in our standard mixes are derived purely from:
  - **Traditional Processing:** Washing, sun-drying, and slow roasting naturally break down complex starches into simpler sugars, enhancing natural sweetness. (For sprouted single-grain flours, germination naturally unlocks maltose).
  - **Spices:** Green cardamom gives a sweet sensory illusion.
  - **Dates:** Dried dates ground into specific mixes provide a mild baseline sweetness.

---

## 2. Recommended Sweeteners (To Be Added by Customers)

If customers prefer a sweeter taste for children or personal preference, we recommend adding these whole, natural sweeteners:

### 1. Organic Jaggery (Gur)
- **What it is:** Unrefined cane sugar juice, solidified.
- **Why use it:** Rich in iron and molasses minerals. It is processed without sulfur chemical bleaching.
- **How to use:** Add 1 teaspoon of organic jaggery powder to the porridge after turning off the flame.

### 2. Raw Honey
- **What it is:** Pure, unfiltered honey.
- **Why use it:** Loaded with active enzymes, antioxidants, and anti-inflammatory properties.
- **How to use:** Let the porridge cool slightly before stirring in honey. *Crucial: Never heat or cook honey, as high heat destroys its beneficial enzymes.*

### 3. Date Paste / Powder
- **What it is:** Dried, finely ground dates.
- **Why use it:** Provides dietary fiber, iron, and a slow-release natural sugar profile that does not cause sudden insulin spikes.
- **How to use:** Whisk date powder into the milk along with the Nutrimix before cooking, or blend raw dates into smoothies.
