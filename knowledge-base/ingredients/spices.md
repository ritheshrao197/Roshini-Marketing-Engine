# Ingredient Directory: Natural Spices

We use traditional spices to add natural aroma and flavor to our health mixes without resorting to synthetic vanilla, chocolate, or artificial strawberry flavoring agents.

---

## 1. Green Cardamom (Elaichi)
- **Sourcing:** Premium green cardamom pods sourced from Idukki, Kerala, for high essential oil content.
- **Key Nutrients:** Rich in natural essential oils (Cineole, Terpinene, Pinene) and antioxidants.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Digestive Catalyst:** Stimulates salivary glands and stomach acid production, facilitating faster, cleaner digestion.
  - **Prevents Bloating:** Natural carminative properties prevent gas and bloating, making millets easier on sensitive infant and senior stomachs.
  - **Aroma Therapy:** Provides a soothing, sweet aroma that masks the earthy flavor of raw millets, making the health mix highly palatable to toddlers.
  - **Breath Freshener:** Cleanses oral bacteria naturally.
