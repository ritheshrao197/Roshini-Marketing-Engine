# Ingredient Directory: Superfood Seeds

Our health mixes incorporate a selection of nutrient-dense seeds, providing essential minerals, high-quality plant protein, and anti-inflammatory Omega-3 fatty acids.

---

## 1. Pumpkin Seeds
- **Key Nutrients:** Exceptionally rich in Zinc, Magnesium, Iron, and plant-based protein.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Immunity Booster:** High zinc concentration is vital for cell-mediated immunity and wound healing.
  - **Deep Sleep & Recovery:** Magnesium helps relax muscles, supports heart health, and improves sleep quality.
  - **Hormonal Regulation:** Supports healthy endocrine functions.

## 2. Flax Seeds (Alsi)
- **Key Nutrients:** Rich in Alpha-Linolenic Acid (ALA Omega-3), dietary lignans, and soluble/insoluble fiber.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Heart & Blood Vessels:** ALA Omega-3 reduces inflammation and helps maintain healthy blood pressure.
  - **Hormonal Balance:** Lignans have antioxidant properties that help stabilize endocrine balances.
  - **Digestive Motility:** Mucilage fiber keeps the gut clean and prevents constipation.

## 3. Chia Seeds
- **Key Nutrients:** Calcium, Phosphorus, plant-based Omega-3, and soluble fiber.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Bone Strength:** High calcium and phosphorus content work alongside sprouted Ragi for skeletal support.
  - **Hydration & Satiety:** Absorbs water, swelling in the gut to promote a prolonged feeling of fullness (satiety).

## 4. Watermelon Seeds
- **Key Nutrients:** Magnesium, Iron, Zinc, and healthy monounsaturated/polyunsaturated fats.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Metabolic Function:** Magnesium assists over 300 enzymatic reactions in the body.
  - **Skin & Coat Health:** Natural oils support skin barrier functions and cell growth.
