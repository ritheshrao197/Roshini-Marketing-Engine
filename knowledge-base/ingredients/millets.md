# Ingredient Directory: Grains & Millets (100% Sprouted)

Millets are ancient grains that have been staples in Indian kitchens for thousands of years. They are naturally gluten-free, drought-resistant, and packed with dense nutrition. In Roshini's products, every millet is 100% sprouted to maximize nutrient absorption and ease digestion.

---

## 1. Sprouted Finger Millet (Ragi / Nachni)
- **Primary Role:** Serves as the high-calcium base for our health mixes.
- **Key Nutrients:** Exceptionally high in Calcium, dietary fiber, iron, and amino acids (L-Lysine and L-Methionine).
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Bone Density:** Vital for bone formation in growing toddlers and maintaining bone density in senior citizens.
  - **Anemia Prevention:** Rich in iron to assist red blood cell production.
  - **Bioavailability:** Sprouting increases Ragi's bioavailable calcium content by up to 3x, making it highly absorbable.

## 2. Sprouted Pearl Millet (Bajra / Kambu)
- **Primary Role:** Natural energy booster and mineral source.
- **Key Nutrients:** Iron, Magnesium, Zinc, Potassium, and soluble fiber.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Heart Health:** High magnesium supports healthy cardiovascular and muscle functions.
  - **Sustained Energy:** High energy density keeps the body warm and active.
  - **Blood Sugar Regulation:** Slow-burning complex carbs help prevent sudden glucose spikes.

## 3. Sprouted Sorghum (Jowar / Cholam)
- **Primary Role:** Gut wellness and light digestion.
- **Key Nutrients:** Calcium, Copper, dietary fiber, and plant protein.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Digestive Ease:** Extremely gentle on the gut, making it perfect for babies and elders.
  - **Immunity Boost:** Loaded with phytochemical antioxidants that support cellular defense.
  - **Cholesterol Management:** Soluble fiber helps maintain healthy lipid levels.

## 4. Sprouted Foxtail Millet (Kangni / Tenai)
- **Primary Role:** Micronutrient enrichment and metabolism support.
- **Key Nutrients:** Thiamine (Vitamin B1), Niacin, and dietary fiber.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Nervous System:** Supports healthy nerve signaling and brain function.
  - **Digestion:** High fiber content helps regulate bowel movements and supports weight control.

## 5. Sprouted Little Millet (Kutki / Samai)
- **Primary Role:** Mineral replacement and cellular health.
- **Key Nutrients:** Rich in iron, essential fatty acids, and B-vitamins.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Energy Metabolism:** Vitamin B complexes help convert food into usable energy.
  - **General Strength:** High zinc and magnesium support overall immunity.

## 6. Sprouted Barnyard Millet (Sanwa / Kuthiraivali)
- **Primary Role:** Blood sugar management and dietary fiber.
- **Key Nutrients:** High fiber, low glycemic index, and essential amino acids.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Low Glycemic Load:** Slower digestion rate, making it highly recommended for diabetic wellness.
  - **Satiety:** Keeps you feeling full for longer, reducing snack cravings.

## 7. Sprouted Brown Top Millet (Korle)
- **Primary Role:** Antioxidant defense and detox.
- **Key Nutrients:** High concentration of dietary fiber and natural antioxidants.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Gut Cleansing:** Acts as a prebiotic to feed healthy gut bacteria.
  - **Cell Protection:** Fights oxidative stress and free radical damage.

## 8. Sprouted Proso Millet (Chena / Barri)
- **Primary Role:** Plant protein and nervous health.
- **Key Nutrients:** High protein content, lecithin, and essential amino acids.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Tissue Growth:** Supports cellular growth and repair.
  - **Nervous System:** Lecithin supports healthy neural structures and cognitive function.

## 9. Sprouted Kodo Millet (Kodon / Varagu)
- **Primary Role:** Anti-inflammatory and blood pressure wellness.
- **Key Nutrients:** Polyphenols, fiber, and calcium.
- **Health Benefits:**
  - **Immunity Support:** Helps reduce systemic inflammation.
  - **Heart Wellness:** Supports clean arteries and steady blood circulation.
