# Health Claims & Regulatory Compliance Guidelines

This compliance sheet is a **critical ruleset** for every AI content agent. It ensures all generated copy aligns with FSSAI advertising regulations and ethical food-labeling standards.

---

## 1. Compliance Ruleset: Always Say vs. Never Say

When generating copy for social media, blogs, emails, packaging labels, or WhatsApp statuses, adhere strictly to the following phrasing:

### What to ALWAYS SAY (Approved Claims)
- "Supports a healthy and active lifestyle."
- "A good source of dietary fiber, promoting healthy digestion."
- "Rich in plant-based protein from natural nuts and seeds."
- "Contains 100% natural, clean ingredients."
- "Prepared using premium sprouted millets and nuts."
- "A natural source of iron and bioavailable calcium."
- "Contains healthy unsaturated fats (Omega-3 and Omega-6)."
- "Supports balanced, wholefood nutrition for the entire family."
- "Helps maintain sustained energy levels without sugar crashes."
- "Gentle on sensitive or growing stomachs."

### What to NEVER SAY (Forbidden Claims)
- **Do NOT claim that our products "cure" or "treat" diseases:**
  - *Never say:* "Cures diabetes" or "Reverses type-2 diabetes."
  - *Never say:* "Treats cancer" or "Fights cancer."
  - *Never say:* "Prevents heart disease" or "Cures hypertension."
- **Do NOT guarantee physical results:**
  - *Never say:* "Guaranteed weight loss in 30 days" or "Causes quick fat loss."
  - *Never say:* "Boosts child's IQ" or "Increases brain intelligence."
- **Do NOT claim clinical or medical endorsement unless fully certified:**
  - *Never say:* "Clinically proven formula" (unless backed by specific lab trial results).
  - *Never say:* "Doctor recommended" or "Pediatrician verified" (unless explicitly verified and documented).
  - *Never say:* "Acts as a replacement for daily medicines."

---

## 2. Ethical Framing Principles

1. **Focus on Support:** Use verbs like *supports*, *helps*, *aids*, *promotes*, and *contributes to* rather than absolute terms like *cures*, *guarantees*, *prevents*, or *fixes*.
2. **Attribute Benefits to Ingredients:** Instead of saying "Nutrimix will strengthen your bones," say "Sprouted Ragi is naturally rich in calcium, which supports bone density and strength."
3. **Be Transparent About Allergens:** Always state that the product contains tree nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts, pistachios) and peanuts. Never hide potential allergens in marketing copy.
