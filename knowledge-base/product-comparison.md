## 1. Direct Feature Comparison

| Feature | Roshini's Homemade Nutrimix | Commercial "Health" Porridges & Drinks |
|---|---|---|
| **Processing Method** | **100% Sprouted:** 12-hour germination activates phytase to break down anti-nutrients. | **Raw/Unsprouted:** Grains are ground directly, leaving phytic acid intact (blocks mineral absorption). |
| **Batch Size** | **Small Home Batches:** Roasted and ground in 5–10 kg lots to order. Maximum freshness. | **Mass Industrial Production:** Prepared in tons, stored in warehouses for months. |
| **Added Sugars** | **0% Added Sugar:** Purely natural sweetness from sprouted grains, cardamoms, and dates. | **High Sugar (Up to 30%):** Loaded with refined sugar, sucrose, liquid glucose, or maltodextrin. |
| **Fillers & Additives** | **Zero Fillers:** Made purely from whole grains, nuts, and seeds. | **High Fillers:** Contains corn starch, wheat flour fillers, maltodextrin, and chemical thickeners. |
| **Preservatives & Colors** | **100% Natural:** No artificial colors, preservatives, or chemical shelf-stabilizers. | **Chemical Additives:** Uses Class-II preservatives, emulsifiers, and synthetic flavor/color. |
| **Nut Quality** | **Premium Nuts:** Mamra almonds, high-oil walnuts, pumpkin seeds, watermelon seeds. | **Cheap Fillers:** Minimal nut content, often relying on peanut skins or artificial nut flavoring. |
| **Milling Process** | **Slow Stone Grinding:** Prevents high heat, preserving heat-sensitive vitamins and enzymes. | **High-Speed Industrial Milling:** Generates high heat that can denature nutrients and oils. |
| **Age Suitability** | **Whole Family:** Suitable for infants (6+ months), active adults, pregnant women, and diabetics. | **Age-Restricted:** Segmented artificially, often too high in sugar for diabetics or toddlers. |
