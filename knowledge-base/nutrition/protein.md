# Nutrition Directory: Plant Protein

Protein is the building block of life, essential for muscle repair, bone density, cellular growth, and hormone production. Roshini's Home Products utilize a blend of grains, nuts, and seeds to deliver clean, wholefood plant protein.

---

## 1. Natural Protein Sources in Our Mixes

Unlike commercial health shakes that use isolated protein powders (like whey or soy isolates processed with chemicals), our products deliver wholefood protein from:
- **Ancient Grains:** Sprouted Proso millet, Pearl millet (Bajra), and Finger millet (Ragi).
- **Premium Nuts:** Peanuts, Almonds, Cashews, Pistachios, and Walnuts.
- **Superfood Seeds:** Pumpkin seeds, Watermelon seeds, Flax seeds, and Chia seeds.

---

## 2. Key Health Benefits of Our Wholefood Protein

### 1. Muscle Repair & Recovery
- Rich in plant-derived amino acids that repair micro-tears in muscles after exercise, making it ideal for fitness enthusiasts and gym goers.

### 2. Growth & Development in Children
- Provides the essential structural blocks needed for tissue growth, skeletal development, and immune antibody formation in kids.

### 3. Satiety & Weight Management
- Protein stimulates the release of satiety hormones (like peptide YY and CCK) while reducing hunger hormones (ghrelin). This keeps you feeling full and satisfied for longer, preventing mid-morning snack cravings.

### 4. Vegetarian & Vegan Diet Support
- Provides a clean, daily, multi-source protein supplement for families pursuing a vegetarian or vegan lifestyle.
