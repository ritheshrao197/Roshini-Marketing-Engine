# Nutrition Directory: Antioxidants

Antioxidants are natural compounds that protect cells from oxidative stress and free radical damage. Roshini's Home Products are packed with natural plant-based antioxidants, which help reduce systemic inflammation and support overall cellular health.

---

## 1. Natural Antioxidants in Our Ingredients

Our ingredients are selected not just for macronutrients (carbs, protein, fat), but also for their protective phytochemicals:

### Polyphenols & Tannins
- **Primary Source:** Barnyard millet, Kodo millet, Finger millet (Ragi), and Sorghum (Jowar).
- **Benefits:** These phytochemicals possess strong anti-inflammatory properties and help protect vascular health.

### Lignans
- **Primary Source:** Flax seeds and sesame seeds.
- **Benefits:** A type of phytoestrogen that acts as a strong antioxidant, helping stabilize hormonal balance in women.

### Vitamin E (Tocopherols)
- **Primary Source:** Mamra almonds, walnuts, and pumpkin seeds.
- **Benefits:** A fat-soluble antioxidant that protects lipid membranes in brain cells and skin tissues from free radical degradation.

### Lutein & Zeaxanthin
- **Primary Source:** Green pistachios.
- **Benefits:** Carotenoid antioxidants that accumulate in the retina, protecting eyes from oxidative stress and blue light damage.

---

## 2. Health & Wellness Benefits

- **Anti-Inflammatory Action:** Systemic inflammation is a root cause of joint pain and metabolic fatigue. Antioxidants help neutralize inflammatory free radicals.
- **Cellular Protection:** Supports healthy cell division and repair across the body.
- **Brain Health Support:** The brain is highly vulnerable to oxidative damage due to its high oxygen usage. ALA Omega-3 (from walnuts) and Vitamin E (from almonds) work together to protect brain cell membranes.
