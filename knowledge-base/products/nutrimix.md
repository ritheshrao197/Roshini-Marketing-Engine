## 5. Preparation & Recipes

### Classic Millet Malt (Porridge)
*A comforting, warm, daily breakfast bowl.*
1. Mix **2 tablespoons** of Nutrimix powder with 1 cup (250ml) of cold milk or water in a saucepan.
2. Whisk thoroughly to dissolve all lumps *before* heating.
3. Cook on a low-medium flame for **5 to 7 minutes**, stirring constantly as it thickens.
4. Turn off the flame. Optionally sweeten with a teaspoon of jaggery, honey, or date paste. Garnish with chopped dry fruits and serve hot.

### Instant Protein Smoothie
*A fast, raw, cold shake requiring no cooking.*
1. Blend **2 tablespoons** of Nutrimix (dry-roasted for 2 minutes and cooled) with 1 ripe banana, 3 soaked dates, and a glass (250ml) of cold milk (dairy or plant-based).
2. Blend on high for 1 minute until smooth. Serve cold.
