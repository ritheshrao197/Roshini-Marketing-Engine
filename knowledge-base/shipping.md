# Shipping & Delivery Policies

This document serves as the source of truth for all delivery timelines, rates, and shipping policies used across customer communication channels (WhatsApp, email, and social media).

---

## 1. Shipping Rates & Limits

- **Free Shipping Threshold:** Free delivery across India on all orders **above ₹500**.
- **Standard Shipping Fee:** Flat **₹40** for orders below ₹500.
- **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Available in select pin codes for an additional fee of **₹30** (to cover courier handling fees).

---

## 2. Dispatch Timeline

- **Freshness Promise:** Since we roast, grind, and pack fresh in small home batches, we never ship stale stock. 
- **Processing Time:** Orders are typically processed and dispatched within **24 to 48 hours** of payment/order confirmation.
- **Holidays:** Dispatches are made Monday through Saturday. No dispatches are processed on Sundays or national holidays.

---

## 3. Delivery Timelines

Once dispatched from our home facility, the estimated transit times are as follows:

| Region | Delivery Timeline |
|---|---|
| **Metro Cities** (Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Delhi, etc.) | **3 to 5 business days** |
| **Non-Metro Cities & Tier-2/3 Towns** | **5 to 7 business days** |
| **Northeast & Remote Locations** | **7 to 10 business days** |

---

## 4. Tracking & Support

- **Tracking Links:** Customers receive a tracking number and link via SMS and WhatsApp as soon as the courier partner scans the shipment.
- **Courier Partners:** We partner with reliable delivery networks like Delhivery, Blue Dart, and Speed Post to ensure safe handling.
- **Support:** For delayed shipments or delivery issues, customers can contact us directly at support@roshinishomeproducts.com or via WhatsApp support.
