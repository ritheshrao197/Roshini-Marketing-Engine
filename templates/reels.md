# Short-Form Video (Reels & Shorts) Scripts Template

Use this blueprint to structure all 30-second and 60-second video scripts, visuals, text overlays, and audio queues.

---

## 1. 30-Second Reel Script Grid Layout

Every reel script must follow this structural layout:

| Time | Visual Scene (Camera direction, action) | Voiceover (Audio script) | On-Screen Text Overlay (Poppins Bold) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0 - 3s (The Hook)** | Quick movement. Showing a raw ingredient or a problem state (e.g. dumping sugary cereal). | A highly engaging question or shocking statement. | *"Stop buying this slop! ❌"* or *"Calcium deficiency? Check this out."* |
| **3 - 15s (The Process / Science)** | Close-up shots of slow home roasting, sprouted ragi grains rising, or blending a thick smoothie. | The core educational value. Keep it fast, active, and conversational. | *"Sprouting multiplies nutrition by 3x"* |
| **15 - 25s (The Result)** | A steaming cup of ragi malt, or a happy child eating. Show textures and steam. | How the product fits in naturally. Describe the aroma and taste. | *"100% natural. Zero added sugar."* |
| **25 - 30s (Call to Action)** | Zoom in on the Nutrimix pouch, showing the hand-roasted quality label. | Direct instruction to tap the bio link or reply to the video. | *"Tap the link in bio to order yours fresh! 🛒"* |

---

## 2. Dynamic Video Hooks Library
Select and rotate these hooks for video intro sequences:

- **The Problem Hook:** *"Are you still buying standard baby foods with 30% added sugar?"*
- **The Curiosity Hook:** *"This one traditional habit from our grandmothers is the ultimate modern breakfast hack."*
- **The Science Hook:** *"Why raw grains are blocking your body's calcium absorption (and how sprouting fixes it)."*
- **The Quick-Prep Hook:** *"The healthiest breakfast you'll ever make in under 180 seconds."*

---

## 3. Video Editing & Audio Guidelines
- **Background Audio:** Upbeat, natural acoustic guitar, cheerful folk, or gentle morning piano tracks. No harsh electronic music.
- **On-Screen Captions:** Words should appear one-by-one or line-by-line as the voiceover speaks, colored in primary green (`#4E7A2E`) or accent gold (`#D98C2B`) to match the brand guide.
- **Pacing:** Transition visual clips every 2 to 3 seconds to keep viewer attention high.
