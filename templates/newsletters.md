# Email Newsletter Templates

Use these structures to generate weekly and monthly marketing newsletters for Roshini's Home Products subscribers.

---

## 1. Weekly Newsletter Template (Wholesome Wisdom)

### Metadata
- **Preheader Text:** (50-80 characters, supporting the subject line with a summary hook)
- **Subject Line:** (Under 50 characters, curiosity-gap style, warm and personal)
  - *Examples:* *"The calcium source your kitchen is missing"*, *"A 3-minute breakfast hack for busy moms"*, *"Why sprouting changes everything"*

### Email Body Sections

#### 1. Header & Greeting
- Warm, personal opening (e.g. *"Hey [First Name],"*).
- Short intro acknowledging the day of the week or current season.

#### 2. The Educational Focus (Top Story)
- 2 to 3 short paragraphs detailing one health/nutrition insight (e.g. why sugar crashes happen, the importance of iron for school children, sprouted digestibility). Keep the paragraphs short (2-3 sentences each) for easy mobile readability.

#### 3. Recipe or Health Tip of the Week
- Introduce one simple recipe using Nutrimix.
- Format with simple list markers:
  - 🥣 **Recipe name:** Brief 1-sentence intro.
  - 🛒 **Ingredients:** List key ingredients.
  - ⚡ **Why it works:** Focus on the nutritional benefit.

#### 4. Product Spotlight & Customer Testimonial
- Share a real review from a mother or senior citizen (refer to `knowledge-base/customer-personas.md` or reviews database).
- Display the testimonial in blockquote style using *Playfair Display (Italic)*.
- Highlight a product package link with a direct button/text CTA.

#### 5. Sign-off
- Warm sign-off (e.g. *"To your family's health, <br> Roshini"*).
- **P.S. Section:** Keep it high conversion. E.g. *"P.S. Fresh batch is being ground tomorrow morning! Order now to get yours dispatched in time. Use code **FIRST10** if it's your first pouch."*
