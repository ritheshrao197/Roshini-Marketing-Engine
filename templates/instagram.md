# Instagram Post & Grid Templates

Use these structures and guidelines to draft all Instagram feed posts, carousels, and visual briefs.

---

## 1. Static/Single Image Post Template

### Copy Structure
- **Line 1 (Hook):** Direct, punchy, bold hook statement. E.g. *"This 1 grain has 3x more calcium than milk. 🤯"*
- **Paragraph 1 (Problem/Context):** Highlight a common family struggle (time, nutrition, sugar slumps).
- **Paragraph 2 (Solution/Insight):** Explain how Sprouted Millets or Nutrimix solve this problem naturally.
- **CTA:** Directed line pointing to the bio link or direct message. E.g. *"Click the link in our bio to order a fresh, home-roasted pouch today! 🛒"*
- **Hashtags:** (Rotate using `history/hashtags.md`).

### Visual Brief (For Designer / Canva)
- **Background Color:** Warm Sand (`#FFF8EE`).
- **Visual Element:** Flat lay of sprouted ragi grain bowls next to almond kernels.
- **On-Image Text:** Big bold Poppins Bold text: *"The Sprouting Magic"* or *"Calcium Superfood"*.
- **Icon Placement:** Hand-drawn checkmarks (`#5FA146`) showing benefits.

## 2. Multi-Slide Carousel Template (The Education Funnel)
*Best for Millet Monday or Nutrition Tuesday.*

- **Slide 1: The Hook Cover**
  - *Headline:* Poppins Bold, large. E.g. *"The Truth About Kids' Breakfasts"*
  - *Subheading:* Playfair Display. *"Why modern cereals fail, and traditional grains win."*
  - *Visual:* Split image: Box of sugar loops vs. a clay bowl of Nutrimix porridge.
- **Slides 2 – 4: The Core Insights**
  - *Content:* One benefit per slide, structured with clear, simple icons and bullet points (e.g. Slide 2: Calcium, Slide 3: Digestion, Slide 4: Steady Energy).
- **Slide 5: The Product Pivot**
  - *Headline:* *"Wholesome Goodness, Made Simple"*
  - *Copy:* Introduce Nutrimix, highlighting 100% natural, sprouted, and no sugar.
- **Slide 6: Call to Action**
  - *Headline:* *"Tap the Bio Link to Order!"*
  - *Offer Highlight:* ₹249 starter trial pack or FIRST10 discount code.

## 3. Visual Image Prompts for AI Generation
When generating matched image assets, sub-agents should output prompts matching this style:

- **Aesthetic Guidance:** Pure, natural lighting, organic textures (wood/linen), clear focus on key ingredients (ragi, almonds, walnuts).
- **Prompt Template:**  
  `Minimalist aesthetic, a clay cup of thick ragi malt on a warm wooden table. A few raw walnuts and almonds scattered near the cup. Soft morning sun casting long gentle shadows. Professional food styling, organic vibe, warm color tones, 8k.`
