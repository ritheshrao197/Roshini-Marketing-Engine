# Product Launch Campaign Frameworks

Use this copywriting blueprint to coordinate social media, email newsletters, and WhatsApp sequences when introducing new products (e.g. customized kids mixes, millet cookies, dry fruit health bars, or seasonal variants).

---

## 1. Phase 1: The Teaser Sequence (7 Days Out)
- **Objective:** Build anticipation and curiosity around a new product.
- **Copy Hooks:** 
  - *"We've been secretly baking something sprouted..."*
  - *"Can you guess what happens when you combine sprouted ragi with raw cocoa?"*
- **Actionable Call-to-Action:** "Reply with your guess to get early access to the secret preorder list!"
- **Platform Strategy:** Instagram Story polls, short Reel sneak-peeks, and WhatsApp Status teasers.

---

## 2. Phase 2: Pre-Order Launch Sequence (3 Days Out)
- **Objective:** Convert highly engaged followers/leads into pre-orders.
- **Offer:** Exclusive pre-order discount: **15% OFF** for the first 50 orders using a custom code.
- **Promo Code:** **EARLYBIRD**
- **Core Messaging:** Focus on the small-batch, handmade nature of the product. Explain that capacity is limited because everything is made fresh at home.
- **Platform Strategy:** Direct email to newsletter list and WhatsApp Broadcast.

---

## 3. Phase 3: Official Launch Day Sequence (Day 0)
- **Objective:** Drive maximum traffic to the product landing page and social media shops.
- **Structure:**
  - **The Hook:** High-energy announcement. E.g. *"It's finally here! Meet the next generation of healthy family snacks."*
  - **The USP Breakdown:** Detail the premium ingredients, the hygiene protocols, FSSAI compliance, and who it is for.
  - **The Customer Guarantee:** "Freshly roasted, ground, and dispatched to order."
  - **Promo Code:** **FIRST10** (for new buyers) or **WELCOMEBACK** (for repeat customers).
- **Platform Strategy:** Heavy social media posting, video walkthroughs of packing the first orders, and email blasts.

---

## 4. Phase 4: Social Proof & Review Follow-Up (14 Days Post-Launch)
- **Objective:** Gather testimonials to build long-term trust.
- **Messaging:** Reach out via WhatsApp or personal email asking for honest feedback.
- **Copy Angle:** "We want to make sure your children loved the taste of our new millet cookies. Drop us a review, and we'll send a ₹50 gift coupon for your next batch."
