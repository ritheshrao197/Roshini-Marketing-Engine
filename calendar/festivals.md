# 2026 Indian Festival Marketing Triggers

Use this calendar to automatically inject seasonal greeting hooks and promotional messaging into the content engines.

---

## Q1 Festivals (January – March)

### Makar Sankranti / Pongal / Lohri (Mid-January)
- **Focus Grain:** Sesame seeds, Sprouted Jowar, and Bajra (warm-giving millets).
- **Core Theme:** Harvesting, agricultural heritage, and traditional sweets.
- **Copy Hook:** "Celebrate the harvest with the grains of our ancestors. Pure, sprouted, and nourishing."
- **Offer Angle:** Buy 2 get 1 free on 250g packs to encourage healthy holiday gifting.

### Maha Shivratri (Late February / Early March)
- **Focus Grain:** Ragi (Finger Millet) malt.
- **Core Theme:** Fasting nutrition (Sustained energy during fasts).
- **Copy Hook:** "Fuel your fast with slow-release energy. Gluten-free Sprouted Ragi malt keeps you full and focused."
- **Offer Angle:** Free sample pack of pumpkin seeds with 500g purchases.

## Q2 Festivals (April – June)

### Ugadi / Gudi Padwa / Puthandu (Late March / Mid-April)
- **Focus Grain:** Nutrimix sweet porridge (using cardamom).
- **Core Theme:** New beginnings, spring rejuvenation, detoxing.
- **Copy Hook:** "A sweet, healthy start to the new year. Pure homemade goodness for a healthier family."
- **Offer Angle:** Launch of the "Family Wellness Combo" (discounted).

### Mother’s Day (Second Sunday of May)
- **Core Theme:** Caring for the mother, maternal health, pregnancy nutrition.
- **Copy Hook:** "Nourishing the ones who nourish us. Give your mother the gift of raw, sprouted health."
- **Offer Angle:** "Mother’s Health Combo" — 10% off using code **MOMHEALTH**.

## Q3 Festivals (July – September)

### Back to School Season (June – July)
- **Core Theme:** Kid's brain development, healthy school lunchbox additions.
- **Copy Hook:** "Ditch the packaged sugar. Send them to school with natural brain food (Almonds + Walnuts + Sprouted Ragi)."
- **Offer Angle:** Combo packs with free kid-friendly recipe cards.

### Ganesh Chaturthi (August / September)
- **Focus Grain:** Sprouted millet modak/laddus.
- **Core Theme:** Guilt-free festival sweets, sugar-free celebrations.
- **Copy Hook:** "Traditional sweets made healthy. Prepare your modaks with sprouted Nutrimix and dates."
- **Offer Angle:** Ghee + Nutrimix laddu bundles.

## Q4 Festivals (October – December)

### Navratri / Dussehra (October)
- **Core Theme:** Festive energy, clean eating, fasting energy.
- **Copy Hook:** "Nine days of clean energy. Wholesome grain power for festive rituals."
- **Offer Angle:** Combo packs with free shipping.

### Diwali (October / November)
- **Core Theme:** Healthy gifting, swapping sweets for dry fruit mixes.
- **Copy Hook:** "Gift health this Diwali. Replace processed sweets with premium sprouted nut mixes."
- **Offer Angle:** Festive gift boxing and bulk discounts (FIRST10 code highlight).

### Christmas (December)
- **Core Theme:** Cozy winter mornings, warm ragi cocoa.
- **Copy Hook:** "Warm up your winter mornings with a creamy millet cocoa porridge."
- **Offer Angle:** End-of-year review offer.
