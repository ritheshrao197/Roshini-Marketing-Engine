# Promotional Campaigns Framework

This document outlines the core marketing and sales campaigns executed by the content engines. It specifies target copy structures, triggers, and offer codes.

---

## 1. The Welcome & Trial Campaign
- **Objective:** Convert new website visitors or social media followers into first-time buyers.
- **Offer:** **10% OFF** on the first purchase.
- **Promo Code:** **FIRST10**
- **Core Messaging Focus:** Focus on trust, ingredients transparency, and pure homemade quality. Introduce the 250g pack as the low-barrier entry trial size.
- **Copy Guidelines:** Emphasize "Freshly ground to order" and "No risk trial."

---

## 2. "Share Good Health" Referral Campaign
- **Objective:** Leverage existing satisfied customers (especially mothers) to acquire new customers via word-of-mouth.
- **Offer:** Double-sided discount: Referrer gets a **₹50 coupon** for their next order, and the referred friend gets **₹50 off** their first order.
- **Core Messaging Focus:** Focus on sharing traditional recipes with close friends, parent circles, and neighborhood WhatsApp groups.
- **Copy Guidelines:** "Because good health is meant to be shared. Give ₹50, get ₹50."

---

## 3. Weekend WhatsApp Flash Sales
- **Objective:** Drive quick sales during slow periods using direct WhatsApp Broadcasts.
- **Trigger:** Selected Saturdays (once a month).
- **Offer:** **Free Shipping (saves ₹40)** on any order placed before Sunday midnight, or a flat **₹50 discount** on the Family Wellness Combo.
- **Promo Code:** **WKDWELLNESS**
- **Core Messaging Focus:** High urgency, direct ordering via WhatsApp chat. Focus on stocking up for the upcoming week's breakfast prep.

---

## 4. The "Millet Monday" Educational Funnel
- **Objective:** Create a weekly recurring organic content loop that naturally funnels into product purchases.
- **Trigger:** Every Monday.
- **Format:** A short educational infographic explaining one millet benefit (e.g., calcium in Ragi or iron in Bajra) on Instagram and LinkedIn.
- **Funnel Call-to-Action:** "Get all these sprouted millets and premium nuts in one convenient bag. Tap the link to order."

---

## 5. Festival Gifting Combo Campaign
- **Objective:** Capture bulk orders during Diwali, New Year, and Sankranti.
- **Offer:** Custom festive packaging boxes with free wooden serving spoons.
- **Price Tier:** Combo packs of 3 x 250g pouches at **₹699** (includes gift card).
- **Core Messaging Focus:** Swap processed, high-sugar sweets with wholesome, immunity-boosting dry fruit and millet mixes.
